# IP Roadmap

## 1. Copyright
Source code and documentation are generally protected automatically upon
creation. Preserve authorship notices and maintain dated repository history.

## 2. Trademark
Use trademark strategy for names, logos, and branding. Consider Madrid for
international trademark expansion after your base mark is filed.

## 3. Patent
If you want to protect technical methods or system architecture, evaluate a
patent strategy separately. Madrid does not cover patents.

## 4. Publication Discipline
Do not publish confidential invention details you may want to patent until your
patent strategy is decided.
