# Token Policy

RitualMesh-related tokens are intended to be utility tokens for network use.

They are **not**:
- securities
- investment contracts
- shares of equity
- guarantees of profit
- promises of appreciation

## Intended Utility Uses
- node participation
- service access
- transaction execution
- network resource allocation
- protocol operations

## No Financial Promises
No return, yield, appreciation, or profit is promised or implied.

## Compliance
Any deployment, sale, distribution, or use of tokens must comply with all
applicable laws and regulations in the relevant jurisdiction.
